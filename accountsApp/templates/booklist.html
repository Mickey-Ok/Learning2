A genre of fiction that is set in a particular time period in history and often incorporates real events or people.

Horror..

A genre of fiction that seeks to scare or frighten the reader.

"I have seen the dark universe yawning, where the black planets roll without aim, where they roll in their horror unheeded, without knowledge or lustre or name." - H.P. Lovecraft
